Submit instructions:
The BP proposal submitted to us includes the following contents:
·Project description: describe the project in one sentence
·Product/service: product/service description, including function, performance, structure, intellectual property, etc., and product/service development plan
·Market analysis: your target customers and their characteristics, the size of the market, the company’s current market share, market position
·Competitive landscape: list existing and potential competitors and analyze their competitive advantages;
·Business model: revenue model, pricing, average revenue or value available from each customer, marketing and sales channels, existing and developing customer lists;
·Team description: founder and core management, board members, advisory board members, etc;
·Financial information: a simple financial report of the capital and management operation in the past one or two years, as well as the sales forecast in the next two years, the shareholder structure, the financing plan within one or two years, including the capital demand and the use of capital, etc.
